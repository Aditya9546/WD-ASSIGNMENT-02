Name of the Spot: Manali

Manali is a beautiful hill station nestled in the mountains of Himachal Pradesh, India. It is known for its scenic beauty, adventure sports, and cultural heritage.

Manali - A Serene Landscape

Popularity:

Manali is one of the most popular tourist destinations in India, attracting both domestic and international tourists. It is especially famous among honeymooners and adventure enthusiasts.

Distance from City Center:

The distance from Manali's city center to this picturesque spot is approximately 2 kilometers, making it easily accessible for all visitors.

Best Time to Visit:

The best time to visit Manali is from October to February for snowfall and from March to June for pleasant weather and adventure activities.

Approx Charges:

  • Shared Taxi: ₹500 - ₹800 per person
  • Bus: ₹100 - ₹200 per person
