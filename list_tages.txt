                                      Different  type of lists =: In HTML, a list is a collection of items that are displayed in a specific order, with each item typically preceded by a marker or bullet. Lists are used to present information in a clear and organized manner, making it easier for users to read and understand.



Order list : it will be in order form their are various type of order list they are: 

<ol type="1">        display like :
  <li>Item 1</li>                 1. Item 1       
  <li>Item 2</li>                 3. Item 1
  <li>Item 3</li>                 3. Item 1
</ol>
<ol type="A">        display like:
  <li>Item A</li>                A. Item 1
  <li>Item B</li>                B. Item 1
  <li>Item C</li>                C. Item 1
</ol>
<ol type="I">  - It will we in roman number:
 
 <li>Item 1</li>          display : I. Item 1
  <li>Item 2</li>                  II. Item 2
  <li>Item 3</li>                  III. Item 3
</ol>


unorder list : it will display in unorder form. some of the examples are :
<ul type="disc">       display: • Item 1
<li>Item 1</li>                 • Item 2
  <li>Item 2</li>               • Item 3
  <li>Item 3</li>
</ul>                               

<ul type="square">          display :  ■ Item 1
  <li>Item 1</li>                      ■ Item 2
  <li>Item 2</li>                      ■ Item 3
  <li>Item 3</li>
</ul>

Definition Lists (DL): These are lists that consist of terms and their corresponding definitions. Example:

<dl>
  <dt>Term 1</dt>                          display :  Term 1
  <dd>Definition of Term 1</dd>                           Definition of Term 1
  <dt>Term 2</dt>                                     Term 2  
  <dd>Definition of Term 2</dd>                           Definition of Term 2
</dl>
